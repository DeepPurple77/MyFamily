Memoirs of Aron Jacob Perkel (1882-1975)

"This is being written in the evening in the month of Kislev, in the year 1930."

Life Summary

Aron Jacob Perkel was a master watchmaker whose life spanned continents and historical eras. Born in Adamov, Poland, he survived wars and revolutions through his wit and his trade. After establishing himself in Warsaw, he migrated to Israel in 1924 before eventually settling in South Africa.
